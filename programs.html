   We offer many programs at CT STEM Kids Club.
   The Programs are as followed:
                  Math Courses
  • M-ELEM: A math class for children in grades K-3 teaching a intro to numbers operation and really basic inequalities. The class also teaches fractions, decimals, time, and applications of these topics in the real world. We offer a fun and friendly environment to younger kids.

  • M-ELEM-BeastAcademy: A course involving use of the Beast Academy Series from Art Of Problem Solving. It teaches more advanced topic, such as Geometry, Multiplication etc.. This course is for children in third to fifth grade.

  • M-Prealgebra: A course involving use of the Prealgebra Book from Art Of Problem Solving. Children in this class will learn topics like one step equations, Mean/Median/Mode, and age equations. It is a fun and easy course, and will offer your child a brief intro to mastering Algebra.

  • M-Algebra: A course using the books, Algebra, Geometry, Number Theory, and Probability Books from AOPS. We learn Formulas, and equation solving algorithms, that it's hard to not get anything.

    Science Courses
  • S-Middle School: A course preparing students for high school Science. It teaches topics in Biology and Physics. The class alternates topic every week. In physics children learn topics such as radioactivity, energy, conductors ad insultators and various topics of chemistry. In biology children learn about the various systems of the human body (endocrine, respiratory, etc.).

  • S-Elem-O: A course where students from various grades in elementary school learn many forms of science. We cover weather, speed, motion and force as well as some topics in math. We focus less on traditional classroom methods and more on expirements.


    • Techonology Courses
  • T-HTMLJavaScript: An intro to coding webpages for beginners. It teaches HTML and basic Java Script. They learn tags to make paragraphs, alerts, tables and more. They practice if and for conditional statements and learn the various ways of working with Javascript to create webpages on the internet.

  • T-HTMLJava: An advanced coding group, primarily focused on JAVA. They use a book meant to train you for the official Sun course exam as well as making video games and programming our webpage.


    • Other Courses
  • Vocabulary-Elementary: A course following different levels of the Sadlier Vocabulary Workshop.Sadlie Vocabulary Workshop is a company that makes textbooks for kids to learn vocabulary. The beginner level books range from Red to Blue, and more advanced levels go from A to H.The book for Level Red uses some words that are begginer for little kids, and others which are more advanced. The words are taught through different picture stories of different genres. There is also a set of Common Core words taught as a part of our curriculum. These words are important words from STEM, arts, and sports (even LA can fit into STEM!), and some are words used in standardized testing for elementary grades.
